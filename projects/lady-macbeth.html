Lady Macbeth

Andreas is proud to be part of the ensemble cast in the Norwegian Theatre's (Det Norske Teatret) production of Lady Macbeth, a powerful and dramatic performance that reimagines Shakespeare's classic tragedy.

This production focuses on the complex character of Lady Macbeth, exploring her ambition, guilt, and eventual psychological unraveling. The ensemble work brings new dimensions to this timeless story of power, moral corruption, and the devastating consequences of unchecked ambition.

Working with Det Norske Teatret's acclaimed ensemble, Andreas contributes to a production that challenges traditional interpretations while honoring the dramatic intensity of Shakespeare's original vision. The performance explores themes of gender, power dynamics, and the moral cost of political ambition.
