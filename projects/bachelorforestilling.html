En Bachelorforestilling 2.0: TAP

Featuring Andreas Vedvik – Norwegian Actor

We follow Andreas Vedvik through a journey where he draws out memories from his own life, which is about humiliation and shame. He failed his bachelor performance, his girlfriend has broken up with him, and he has to move back to the countryside.

This deeply personal performance explores themes of failure, rejection, and the struggle to find one's place in the world. Through intimate storytelling and raw emotional honesty, Andreas confronts his own experiences of loss and disappointment.

The performance challenges the audience to reflect on their own experiences of shame and humiliation, creating a space for vulnerability and connection. It asks: What happens when our carefully constructed identities fall apart? How do we rebuild from failure?

Production Details

Performer: Andreas Vedvik

Type: Bachelor Performance

Venue: Westerdals, Høyskolen Kristiania
