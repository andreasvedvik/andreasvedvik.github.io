A Middle Class Hero

Hero Trilogy Part 2 – Andreas Vedvik & Tyrone Douglas

Once again, they have devised a theatre performance exploring middle-class life. What defines the middle class today, and are they content?

The master's students in theater, comparative dramaturgy, and performance studies present seven projects and sketches crafted for the main stage this autumn. These students are nearing the end of their third semester. This marks the second project in a series of larger scenic works they have undertaken during their master's studies.

Key learning objectives in the process have included examining various facets of their forthcoming master's projects and conducting product-oriented foundational work. Collaborating in groups, partnering with both internal and external contributors on concept development and execution, you are invited to experience a woven tapestry of experiments displayed together!
