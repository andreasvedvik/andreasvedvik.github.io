Portfolio

En Bachelorforestilling 2.0: TAP

We follow Andreas Vedvik through a journey where he draws out memories from his own life, which is about humiliation and shame. He failed his bachelor performance, his girlfriend has broken up with him, and he has to move back to the countryside.

A Working Class Hero

A devised theatre performance exploring working-class identity and aspirations through physical theatre and storytelling. Created in collaboration with Tyrone Douglas.

A Middle Class Hero

Once again, they have devised a theatre performance exploring middle-class life. What defines the middle class today, and are they content? The master's students present seven projects and sketches crafted for the main stage.

Lady Macbeth

Andreas is proud to be part of the ensemble cast in the Norwegian Theatre's (Det Norske Teatret) production of Lady Macbeth, a powerful and dramatic performance.

Måken

The actors find themselves in a culture where external values dominate, and everyone yearns to be a star. Starving, they seek external affirmation, chasing a fleeting moment of feeling good enough.

Caligula

A powerful theatrical exploration of power, madness, and the human condition at Det Norske Teatret.

The Dreamer

The third and final installment of the trilogy with Tyrone Douglas, exploring dreams, aspirations, and the human spirit.

NO-WAY

Andreas's debut production, directed by Rudolf Terland Bjørnerem in Stine Indergård's production, marking the beginning of his professional acting career.
